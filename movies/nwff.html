TWO SHORT FILMS COMMISSIONED BY NWFF, 2013

---

The Northwest Film Forum in Seattle commissioned both Nandan and I (Zach) to make a short film each.

---

Nandan's film ($500 OF PARTY) is a semi-crowdsourced craigslist experiment about what's worth celebrating.

My film (THE OWNER OF A SILVERHATCHBACK) is a low stakes whodunnit shot at a show in my basement.
